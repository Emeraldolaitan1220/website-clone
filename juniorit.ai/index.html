Frequently Asked Questions

I am wondering how I can master multiple coding languages and technologies in just a few months?

We don't teach one specific coding language but focus on the concepts and principles behind coding languages. For example, while we are discussing JavaScript, you will also learn the fundamental concepts of assembly language. The goal is to enable you to pick up any coding language you need with the assistance of ChatGPT at any time.

What's JuniorIT.AI's mentorship?

The JuniorIT.AI mentorship program aims to simulate a one-on-one private mentoring environment with real-time interactive practices guided by instructors. In this program, JuniorIT.AI acts as your technical mentor, providing guidance, advice, and support to enhance your coding skills and knowledge. Think of it as working on a shared project, where JuniorIT.AI serves as your technical team leader and project manager

Can I skip some classes in JuniorIT.AI that I already have knowledge of?

We do not recommend skipping any classes or lessons since each one is designed not only to impart knowledge and coding concepts but also to develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and research abilities. While some courses may cover familiar knowledge or concepts, they offer unique benefits from different perspectives that can enhance your overall skills.

What are the differences between JuniorIT.AI's classes and other coding classes or online videos?

You won't find any classes in traditional schools or online that are quite like ours. At JuniorIT.AI, we not only teach coding skills, but we also focus on efficiently using tools like Google and ChatGPT to tackle problems and conduct research. Technology evolves rapidly, but the practical skills you learn from JuniorIT.AI will never become outdated; instead, they will continually improve day by day.

It seems really overwhelming that you list all the technologies on your website. How can I learn them in a short time?

Different technologies are actually very similar. All you need to do is master the concepts and understand what each technology can do. Once you pick up one, you can easily research with Google or ChatGPT to find the details you need. That's why we don't focus on teaching you to memorize specific knowledge; instead, we emphasize understanding the concepts and the context behind these technologies. This approach makes it much easier to quickly learn and understand.
